Get to know us

Flickinger Glassworks sets the standard for quality, precision, and beauty in bent glass through empowering our diverse team of artisans and craftspeople.

Made by hand in

Red Hook,
Brooklyn, NY

Over the years, Flickinger Glass founder Charles built the reputation of Flickinger Glassworks to be a premier glass fabricator specializing in custom glass bending, prototyping, and full-scale high-quality glass production for a number of various uses, from functional to decorative.

About us

Hall of fame

Our expertise in curved glass spans various industries and needs, including windows, lighting solutions, cabinetry, restoration projects, architectural elements, art, and decorative items. Each category benefits from our specialized approach to glass bending, which combines traditional craftsmanship with innovative techniques.
